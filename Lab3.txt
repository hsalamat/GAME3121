Overview: 
Keep your eyes on the road! In this lab you will code a new C# script for your camera, 
which will allow it to follow the vehicle down the road and give the player a proper view of the scene. 
In order to do this, you’ll have to use a very important concept in programming: variables.

Project Outcome:
The camera will follow the vehicle down the road through the scene, allowing the player to see where it’s going. 

New Functionality:
Camera follows the vehicle down the road at a set offset distance

New Concepts & Skills:
Variables 
Data types 
Access Modifiers
Declare and initialize variables
